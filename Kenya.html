The Kenya Project

This project incorporates real world design problems with serious curriculum understanding. This is part of the capstone class Engineering Design and Develpment (EDD).

When Joshua Ngao informed us about the water crisis in his home village of Muamba, our team decided that it was a problem that we wanted to devote our time to. We researched the problem and came up with three different solutions, each of them filtering water using a unique method.

The Kenyan village of Muamba lacks clean water. Current water filtration systems are not efficient or cost effective for the target market. Men, women, and especially children are becoming sick from dirty water consumption. Many people die because of the severity of their disease contracted by the contaminated water.
